Aims&Objectives

EPSF initiatives focus on the areas of public health, pharmacy education, and professional development. Initiatives include multiple public health campaigns, research on issues in pharmacy education and workforce development, the Student Exchange Programme, organising regional events.

Strategic Priority 1

Ensure Excellence in the Operation and Communication of the Federation & Disseminate Scientific and Professional Knowledge.

Strategic Priority 2

Provide Opportunities for Professional Development and International Exchange & Advocate for Improvements to Pharmaceutical Education.

Strategic Priority 3

Encourage the Formation and Development of National Pharmaceutical Student Organizations without Trespassing on Their Domains.

Strategic Priority 4

Provide a Platform for Members to Exchange Knowledge, Experience, and Ideas & Advocate for Improvements to Health Systems.

AboutEPSF

Students Today, Pharmacists Tomorrow!

Egyptian Pharmaceutical Students' Federation (EPSF) is one of the oldest national students’ volunteer organizations; it is Egyptians largest professional non-Governmental, non-political, and non-profitable organization for pharmacy students.
